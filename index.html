Our Story

Born from a Vision of Authentic African Luxury

LeMar – Milo & Aby Resort was conceived as a celebration of Cameroon's extraordinary natural beauty. Situated in Kribi, Africa's hidden coastal gem, our resort invites guests to experience the rare harmony of lush equatorial forest, golden beaches, and the warm Atlantic Ocean.

Whether you arrive as a family seeking adventure, a couple in search of romance, or a solo traveller craving renewal, LeMar is your home away from home — modern in comfort, yet deeply rooted in local culture and tradition.

The Visionary Behind LeMar

Glory Patricia Ivaha Bell Nkomba

Founder & Resort Promoter

Glory Patricia is a dynamic leader in Management and Finance and the visionary force behind one of Kribi's most distinctive hospitality destinations — a thriving oceanfront resort that seamlessly blends luxury, culture, and nature. With a deep passion for innovation and transformative ideas, she has built a multifaceted recreational enterprise that includes a boutique hotel, a rejuvenating spa, a refined restaurant, and an idyllic nature-rich retreat along the pristine shores of the Atlantic Ocean.

Rooted in her native Kribi, Glory Patricia brings both global insight and local authenticity to every aspect of the guest experience. Her keen understanding of today's complex and ever-evolving world enables her to design offerings that are not only elegant and restorative, but also meaningful and culturally grounded. Under her leadership, the resort has become a sanctuary for travelers seeking relaxation, connection, and immersive coastal beauty.

More than a destination, her vision is an invitation — to experience more, to reconnect with nature, and to discover the richness of Kribi through thoughtfully curated hospitality. Glory Patricia is not only a promoter of place, but a partner in creating unforgettable experiences for every guest who walks along the shores she calls home.

Getting Here

Find Your Way to Kribi

LeMar Resort is nestled along the pristine coastline of Kribi, in the South Region of Cameroon — just 165 km south of Douala, accessible by road or private transfer.

  • ✈️
    By Air Douala International Airport (DLA) — 2.5 hrs by road. Private airport transfers available.
  • 🚗
    By Road Via the Edéa–Kribi highway. Resort shuttle service from Douala city on request.
  • ⛵
    By Sea Private boat transfers available from Douala port. Contact concierge for arrangements.
  • 📍
    Address Boulevard du Littoral, Kribi, South Region, Cameroon
