The Pool in All Its Glory

🏊
Main Pool

The Infinity Edge

42 metres of azure water that appears to merge with the Atlantic horizon. Heated to a perfect 32°C year-round, our main pool is suitable for adults and confident swimmers alike.

The pool deck features 30 sun loungers with parasols, and our poolside attendants ensure you want for nothing — from chilled towels to your favourite cocktail.

🧒
Family Zone

The Little Lagoon

Designed exclusively for our youngest guests, the Little Lagoon is a shallow, safe, and beautifully themed pool area featuring gentle water features, a splash pad, and soft poolside seating for parents.

Our trained lifeguards and children's activity coordinators are on duty daily to ensure safety and fun in equal measure.

🌅
Sunset Ritual

The Evening Swim

As the sun touches the horizon of the Gulf of Guinea, our pool takes on a magical golden hue. Join our complimentary guided evening swim at 6:30 PM, followed by cocktails on the upper deck.

This daily ritual has become one of the most beloved experiences at LeMar — a moment of collective beauty that guests return for again and again.

🍹
Poolside Bar

Aqua Bar

Our swim-up bar serves a curated menu of tropical cocktails, fresh juices, light bites, and afternoon snacks. From iced hibiscus punch to classic mojitos and local Cameroon beers — refreshment is always within arm's reach.

Open daily from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Mobile ordering available through our resort app.
