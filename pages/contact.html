How to Reach Us

Getting to LeMar

Kribi is located in the South Region of Cameroon, approximately 165 km south of Douala on the Atlantic coast. Our concierge team is available to arrange seamless transfers from any point of arrival.

  • ✈️
    By Air Fly into Douala International Airport (DLA). Private airport transfers to LeMar take approximately 2.5 hours. Contact us to arrange your transfer in advance.
  • 🚗
    By Road Take the RN7 highway from Douala to Edéa, then continue south on RN3 to Kribi. Resort shuttle departures from Douala available daily at 9:00 AM and 3:00 PM.
  • ⛵
    By Private Transfer We coordinate private vehicle and boat transfers. Contact our concierge at least 48 hours in advance for bespoke arrangements.

Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is by 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out may be arranged subject to availability — please contact us in advance.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Most room rates include daily breakfast at Le Marée Restaurant. Some promotional rates may not include breakfast — this will be clearly indicated at booking.

Do you accommodate families with young children?

Absolutely. Our Family Garden Suites are specially designed for families, and we offer a children's pool, activity coordinators, a kids' menu, and baby amenities upon request.

Is the resort accessible by public transport?

Kribi is accessible via shared bush taxis from Douala. However, we recommend private transfers for comfort. We can arrange transport from any major Cameroonian city.

Can I book the spa or restaurant without staying at the resort?

Yes, day guests are welcome at our spa and restaurant subject to availability. We recommend calling in advance to confirm and reserve your preferred time slot.

Do you host weddings and private events?

LeMar is an exceptional setting for weddings, anniversaries, corporate retreats, and private celebrations. Contact our events team to discuss tailored packages.
