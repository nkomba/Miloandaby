Our Philosophy

Cuisine That Tells a Story

Le Mar is not simply a restaurant — it is a culinary narrative of Cameroon's extraordinary biodiversity. Each dish honours the land, sea, and people of this remarkable country.

Our Executive Chef draws inspiration from the daily catch of Kribi fishermen, organic produce from local farms, and age-old Cameroonian recipes passed down through generations — then elevates them with modern technique and artful plating.

Dine on our oceanfront terrace as the sun sinks below the horizon, or reserve our private chef's table for an immersive seven-course tasting experience.

Our Chef

Chef Aristide Mboa

"Every ingredient has a story. I just help tell it."

Born in Yaoundé and trained at the Cordon Bleu in Paris, Chef Aristide brings three decades of culinary artistry back to his homeland. His menus are a deeply personal love letter to Cameroon — its forests, its coastline, its people, and its flavours.

Having cooked for heads of state and been featured in leading African and French food publications, Chef Aristide now channels his full passion into LeMar's kitchen, where he leads a team of talented local chefs who share his reverence for authentic ingredients and meticulous craft.
